It was a dark and stormy night...

... and the screams of the campers were silenced by the thunderous roars of the storm.

Lightning dashed across the sky two and fro, illuminating the dark red splotches allong the campsite

With a massive THOOM that could be heard for miles, the lightning struck down, incinerating trees and lighting the forest of trees ablaze.

And in synch with the falling tree, the last camper fell lifelessly onto the cold, wet ground.

The inferno quickly jumpred from tree to tree, ingulfing the forest in a deadly blanket of ember and ash.
